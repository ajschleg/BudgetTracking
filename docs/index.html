Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Summary

BudgetTracking is a personal finance application that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share your data with advertisers. Bank connectivity is provided by Plaid Inc.

What we collect

When you connect a bank account via Plaid, BudgetTracking receives from Plaid:

  • Account metadata (name, type, subtype, last 4 digits of the account number, institution)
  • Current and available balances (refreshed only when you request it)
  • Transaction history (description, merchant, amount, date, category)
  • Account owner identity (name, email, phone) as held by your bank

How we store it

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. Plaid access tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with a key stored outside the database. If you enable iCloud sync, an encrypted copy is stored in your own private iCloud account using CloudKit.

Your bank credentials

Your bank username and password go directly to Plaid and are never seen or stored by BudgetTracking. Plaid handles all authentication and maintains the connection to your financial institution. See Plaid's End User Privacy Policy for how Plaid handles your data.

Third parties

  • Plaid Inc. — provides the bank data connection. Governed by your consent at link time and Plaid's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple iCloud — only if you enable sync, and only to your own iCloud account.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — only if you enable the AI insights feature. Aggregated category totals are sent; individual transactions are not.
  • We do not use advertising networks, analytics providers, or data brokers.

Your rights

  • Disconnect a bank at any time from the Settings page. We call Plaid's /item/remove endpoint immediately to revoke access.
  • Disconnect all banks at once from the same Settings page.
  • Delete all data by removing the application and its data from your device (and, if enabled, from iCloud via Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage).
  • Request Plaid data deletion directly from Plaid using my.plaid.com.
