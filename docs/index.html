Privacy Policy

BudgetTracking is a personal finance application that stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers.

When you connect a bank account via Plaid, your credentials are handled entirely by Plaid's secure infrastructure. BudgetTracking receives only transaction data and account balances, which are stored locally on your device. We never have access to your bank login credentials.

You may delete all stored data at any time by removing the application and its associated data from your device. No data is retained after deletion.
